# Delivery Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Documentation and repository setup

Status: current round.

Deliverables:
- master blueprint;
- PRD and frozen MVP;
- architecture and data model;
- UX flows;
- AI and safety specifications;
- business and competition narrative;
- implementation backlog and contribution rules.

Exit condition: repository is ready for disciplined implementation without reopening fundamental scope every day.

## Phase 1 — Foundation

- Initialise Next.js/TypeScript application.
- Configure Supabase environments.
- Add lint, formatting, testing and CI.
- Implement authentication and role onboarding.
- Create migrations, seed data and RLS tests.
- Establish design tokens, layout and accessible components.

Exit condition: users can authenticate into isolated worker, business and admin shells backed by secure persisted data.

## Phase 2 — Business demand

- Business onboarding and verification status.
- Task-template catalogue.
- Appointment-confirmation task creation.
- Moderation checks and task preview.
- Demo payment authorisation state.

Exit condition: a demo business can create and publish a safe, fixed-scope task.

## Phase 3 — Worker readiness and matching

- Capability conversation.
- Structured capability profile.
- Matching and eligibility reason.
- Readiness simulation and rubric feedback.
- Task detail and acceptance snapshot.

Exit condition: a worker can become eligible, practise and accept the demo task.

## Phase 4 — Execution and verification

- Guided workbench.
- Step persistence.
- AI assistance and fallback.
- Evidence submission.
- Business review and one revision.
- Dispute state and admin intervention.

Exit condition: work can move from accepted to approved using real persisted data.

## Phase 5 — Economic proof and progression

- Earnings ledger.
- Demo paid state.
- Verified passport entry.
- Public portfolio controls.
- Experience-summary export.
- Next-opportunity recommendation.

Exit condition: completing work visibly improves the worker's economic and employability record.

## Phase 6 — Competition hardening

- End-to-end tests.
- Security and RLS review.
- Accessibility review.
- Mobile and low-bandwidth optimisation.
- Seeded judge accounts.
- Guided demo journey.
- Analytics and impact dashboard using clearly labelled demo/real data.
- Pitch, video and submission assets.

## Post-MVP validation

Before expanding categories, validate:
- real business willingness to pay;
- worker completion and satisfaction;
- fair effective pay;
- task quality and revision rate;
- dispute/fraud behaviour;
- repeat usage;
- evidence usefulness to employers.

## Expansion order

1. Additional structured admin tasks.
2. Real regulated payment partner.
3. Curated employer and training opportunities.
4. Selected language and voice support after evaluation.
5. Sponsored cohorts and partner dashboards.
6. Geography expansion through local task, legal and language adaptation.

## Non-negotiable sequence

Do not build lending, a wallet, an open marketplace, native apps or USSD before the core work-to-proof loop is functioning and validated.
